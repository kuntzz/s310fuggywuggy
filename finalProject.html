Tu. 11.29.2022 - I want to comment on or recreate escapism, especially what happened for me (and I assume others) through the end of quarantine and re-entering normal life. I feel like I have become out of touch and self-centered, which feels really uncomfortable. I mean I was 20 when it started, freshly out of being a kid. The adult world was new to me and I hadn't navigated on my own before, and with the 2020 quarantine I really was on my own, but the world wasn't how it usually is. But it was. I don't know. At some point it seems like I retreated into myself. Maybe the world didn't make sense because of me/my perception and "mental illnesses." Perhaps I thought if I solved my own mental problems--hacked my brain and rewired it-- that would make the world make more sense. Although I grew more connected to my innerself for some time, ultimately I lost touch with the rest of the world. (It doesn't have to make sense if you just escape it and don't think about it). There's no conclusion yet, I just want to be in touch with people again.

To be brief, I am thinking of something like images or news headlines or videos or sounds of real-world issues that get covered in smoke or psychedellicaly visually warped and swallowed up. Maybe the sounds get distorted until you can't understand them anymore.
